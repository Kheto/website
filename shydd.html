The Bottleworks

This is where Mergin's Succourous Elixir is produced. Mergin holds the secret recipe, although the workers know most of it. He keeps the secret ingredient (synthetic opium) in the chest in his bedroom. The works requires water from the water tower to produce the elixer. The workers are fed up with their conditions and will strike if the works starts bottling again.

The Water Tower

Provides all the water for the town since the well is haunted. It's being guarded by Gus, a thug working for Mergin, in order to ensure water goes to the Bottleworks.

The Well

The previous source of water for the town, currently the shaft is obstructed by a portal to hell. The only way to regain access is to enter the portal and destroy the Satanic Rune.
