ATLFITS

Amulets are short poems whose hashes have three or more 8's in a row. I first heard about them here but the original defininition comes from Robin Sloan. I think I'm kind of ruining the idea by having lots of numbers intead of letters and extra symbols but I like the way it looks. I got all of these ones from the Bible. I wrote a simple Python script to gernerate all the permutations then check their hashes.
